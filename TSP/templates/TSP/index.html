About TSP

Zeitgeist 2022 is proud to announce its second edition of School Outreach Program: Techno School Program(TSP). TSP includes Zeitgeist open Olympiad, Teckwalk and Junior Scientist Competition. Zeitgeist open Olympiad will be a two-phase competition with talented students from prestigious schools participating in two categories namely:
Category 1: Class IX & X
Category 2: Class XI & XII

Zeitgeist Open Olympiad

  • Prize money worth ₹25K will be distributed among the winners of both categories.
  • Registration fee = ₹50
  • Top 25 students will be chosen for the finale.
  • Preliminary test will be conducted in last week of January.
  • Tech walk (Technical Fest and a Campus visit by the students).
  • Exciting cash prizes and goodies for the winner.
  • Certificate of appreciation and vouchers for all students.
  • A special talk regarding career and future by IITIANS and various professionals
  • Finale exam will be in second week of February.


Junior Scientist Competition

  • Registration fee = 100
  • Prize money worth 5K
  • It will be held on second week of February.
  • Maximum of 3 projects are invited from each schools where each projects can be accompanied by 3 students.

About Us


The Techno-Cultural festival of IIT ropar “Zeitgeist”, marks a 3 day journey consisting of various competitions spread across Robotics, Management, Coding, Designing, Gaming, Astronomy, Aero-modelling and Quizzing. Apart from the competitions, talks and workshops are few of the interesting events of the fest. The upcoming Zeitgeist is going to be held in April 2021.
